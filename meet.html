PM Modi concluded his Russia visit and has now departed for Austria. Earlier in the day, Russian President Putin conferred PM Narendra Modi with the country's highest civilian award, the Order of St Andrew the Apostle.

Prime Minister Narendra Modi held bilateral talks with Russian President Vladimir Putin on Tuesday. During the talk PM Modi said, "I am confident that our ties will see further strengthening in coming years" Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Tuesday laid wreath at the Tomb of an unknown soldier in Moscow.

Earlier, Prime Minister Narendra Modi interacted with the Indian diaspora in Moscow said that he has brought the love of 140 crore Indians with him.

Earlier, during his meeting with Russian President Vladimir Putin, Prime Minister Narendra Modi requested the release of Indian soldiers serving in the Russian military amidst the Ukraine conflict.

In his address to the Indian diaspora in Moscow, PM Modi called Russia a "trusted friend" which has always been "India's partner in happiness and sorrow". PM Modi also mentioned the famous Hindi song 'Sar pe laal topi Rusi' in his speech crediting iconic actors Raj Kapoor and Mithun Chakraborty for strengthening the ties between the two countries.

PM Modi landed in Moscow for his two-day Russia visit. PM Modi's visit to Russia marks the first trip since the Russian invasion of Ukraine in February 2022.
